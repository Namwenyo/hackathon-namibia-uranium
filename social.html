Challenges & Opportunities

Balancing Benefits and Concerns
Positive Impacts
  • Job creation in remote areas
  • Skills development
  • Infrastructure improvements
  • Local business opportunities
Challenges
  • Boom-bust cycle dependence
  • Limited female participation
  • Housing shortages
  • Cultural impacts
The Namibian government requires mines to implement Social and Labour Plans (SLPs) that address these challenges through local employment targets, housing programs, and community development initiatives.
